The Future Doctor

Driven by a profound curiosity for the human body and the biological mechanisms of life, my journey in medicine is a pursuit of both science and empathy.

I spend my quiet hours immersed in medical journals and classic sci-fi literature—where the boundaries of what is possible are constantly being tested. From the intricacies of molecular biology to the vast questions of bioethics, I find beauty in the precision of the healing arts.

The Artist

Where medicine requires rigorous logic, art offers a celestial canvas for expression. Drawing is my creative anchor, a way to visualize the intangible.

My illustrations often blend anatomical accuracy with surreal, cosmic elements—star-filled lungs or constellations mirrored in the iris. This balance of science and creativity allows me to approach problems with a unique perspective, seeing patterns where others see chaos.
